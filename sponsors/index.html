HALIFAX GREEK FEST

Why Sponsor Our Festival?

Greek Fest is excited to announce its return for another year of fun! As the longest-running cultural festival in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Greek Fest is a much-anticipated event that celebrates all things Greek.

We anticipate that this year’s ‘Greek Fest Your Way’ will be just as popular as ever, with over 4000 people expected to be served over the weekend. As always, all funds raised will support St. George’s Greek Orthodox Community.

To ensure that everyone can join in on the fun, we’ve made sure to ramp up our promotion efforts on our social channels (FB 7,5k; IG 1547; Email Database of 3k) and greekfest.org. We’ve also invested in digital advertising to reach as many people as possible.

Thank you for your support!
