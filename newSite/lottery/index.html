Draw Details

Ticket prices for the draw are 1 for $2. There is no maximum number of tickets that can be bought. You must be a resident of Nova Scotia to purchase tickets. eTransfers will not be accepted. Deadline to purchase tickets is 6:00 PM on June 11, 2023. Draw will occur on the main stage at the Community Centre at 7:00PM with the winning number announced to the audience. Draws will proceed in order of retail value, beginning with the prize of least value (ie 4th Prize) and continuing in that order until the Grand Prize is awarded. Ballots will be randomly drawn from a box. All prizes, except the Grand Prize, must be accepted as awarded. The Second Prize (Gift Certificate Towards Evolve Fitness) has no cash value. The Grand Prize (Trip for Two to Greece) is limited to a maximum value of $3000. The Community Council will facilitate the purchase of two airfare tickets for dates that are acceptable to the winner and at a maximum price of $3000, or the winner can choose to be awarded cash in lieu of the airfare, at a value of $3000. Winners will be contacted by phone and will continue to be contacted until the prize is claimed.
