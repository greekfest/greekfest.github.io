Wine TastingSaint George Greek Orthodox2025-06-08T23:23:28-03:00

The 2025 Halifax Greek Festival is thrilled to bring back Greek Wine Tasting with local Sommelier and Restaurateur Costa Elles!

Limited spots available! Each session is capped at 12 people, with possible additional sessions pending demand. Don’t miss out!

Join us for an exclusive tasting experience featuring 5 indigenous Greek wine varieties you won’t find anywhere else in Nova Scotia. Costa will take you on a journey through Greece’s renowned wine regions, sharing their rich history and why it can rival many of the well known wine producers of the world.
When: Sunday, June 15, 1pm-2pm
Where: 1146 Purcell’s Cove Rd
Cost: $80 (includes 5 wines, appetizers, and the session with Costa)
To reserve your spot, fill out the following:
https://forms.gle/ywMqVgsvsaAcC6d79
See you there!
